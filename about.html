Travel

Travelling is, without a doubt, my favorite thing to do. Exploring new places opens my mind, helps me see the world differently, and pushes me beyond what’s familiar. Every journey teaches me something—about cultures, people, and myself. I enjoy discovering new environments, trying new things, and understanding how different parts of the world function. For me, travel is not just movement, but growth, and I’m on a long journey to explore as much of the world as I can.

Books

Reading is one of my most valued pursuits beyond academics. I primarily enjoy self-help books, as they offer practical insight and perspective, but I also turn to fiction to immerse myself in entirely new worlds. I’m deeply drawn to the power of words—their ability to articulate ideas, emotions and solutions with clarity. Through reading, I’ve learned effective framing and articulation, and I often find that when navigating an issue or seeking direction, a book provides guidance in a uniquely thoughtful way.
